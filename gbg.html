Part of my bachelor thesis was to create UI/UX that would make possible & confortable to share personal information within IT workgroups.

We are talking about informations that are acquired by mobile phone sensors, for example GPS and location sharing without breaking users privacy.

“...always think about the risk and if breaking the privacy is worth the outcome.”

Jonas Sahlin, Kodamera

“It was crucial to understand users set of mind. Where is the line between breaking the privacy and safely sharing data?”

To solve the privacy issue I did a lot of research, interviews and group discussions. That resulted in theoretical background for application. And so I could start designing User interface for further testing.

The UI proposal was tested by a big group of people and the interface was changed and upgraded based on their feedback. Working prototype was created based in this design.
